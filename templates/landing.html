What is HealSpace?

We built this platform to lower the barrier to mental health support, creating an environment where everyone can find someone who understands.

Community Groups

Post anonymously about what you're going through. Anyone can volunteer to host a group chat to talk about your topic, offer advice, or simply provide a listening ear.

HealBot AI Companion

Our 24/7 AI support assistant is always ready to listen, provide coping strategies, and chat with you securely without judgment.

Certified Professionals

Look for the green Therapist badge. Certified professionals regularly host sessions to offer specialized, expert guidance to the community.

For Licensed Professionals

Lend your expertise to those in need

We are actively seeking licensed clinical social workers, psychologists, and counselors to volunteer their time moderating group chats and helping users.

  • Exclusive Badge: Stand out to users as a verified expert.
  • Host Priority: Claim and host priority support chats.
  • Give Back: Help lower the barrier to mental health care.
How to apply:
  1. Create a standard User Account first via the Login page.
  2. Navigate to your Settings page via the top-right corner.
  3. Scroll down to the "Therapist Verification" section.
  4. Upload copies of your Degree and Government ID for verification.
